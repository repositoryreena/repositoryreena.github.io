hello.

My name is Reena, and I am a front-end developer. I graduated with a BS in Cognitive Science, and I recently obtained a certification in General Assembly's Software Engineer Bootcamp. Drawing inspiration from the simplicity of 90s aesthetics, I bring a nostalgic touch to my projects while leveraging the power of React. With a strong command of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, my expertise lies in developing robust web applications that seamlessly blend modern functionality with the charm of the 90s. By harnessing the capabilities of React, I build dynamic and interactive interfaces that engage users and provide a memorable online experience.
When I'm not immersed in code, I find joy in pursuing a range of creative outlets. I have a passion for baking and experimenting with delicious recipes in the kitchen.
In addition to baking, I enjoy expressing my creativity through drawing and painting. Music is another integral part of my life, as I love playing the piano and singing.
Staying active is important to me, and I often find solace in the water through swimming. To unwind, I indulge in the world of movies and lose myself in captivating stories. Reading is another favorite pastime of mine, as it allows me to explore new worlds and gain fresh perspectives.
I also find fulfillment in giving back to my community through volunteering. Whether it's supporting local organizations or participating in initiatives that make a positive impact, contributing to the greater good is a value I hold dear.
If you're seeking a web developer who can infuse your digital presence with the spirit of the 90s while utilizing the power of React, I'd love to discuss how I can contribute to your project. Let's connect and bring your vision to life!
